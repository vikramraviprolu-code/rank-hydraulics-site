Buyer questions

Practical details that help Rank Hydraulics answer product enquiries quickly.

What details should I send for a hydraulic hose assembly?

Send the hose size or a clear sample photo, pressure rating, media, end fitting type, machine or site context, quantity and urgency.

Can one enquiry cover multiple product lines?

Yes. Share hose, belt, tool, lubricant, coating, welding and VMI requirements together; Rank Hydraulics can route the enquiry by product line.

Which lubricant line is shown on the current site?

The current website uses Veedol as the public lubricant line, reflecting the client-confirmed 2026 update.

What should project or VMI teams include?

Include the site or location, equipment list, recurring products, expected delivery window, urgency level and the site contact person.

Can Rank Hydraulics help with Stauff hydraulic components?

The current material includes Stauff tube fittings, clamps, flanges, gauges, breathers and fluid-power component references.

Are supplier logos formal authorization proof?

The logos identify supplier and product lines referenced by Rank Hydraulics source material. Formal authorization should be confirmed line by line where required.

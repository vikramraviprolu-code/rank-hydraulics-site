## Items I Avoided Or Flagged

- The source site includes generic testimonials and suspicious team names, including "Tony Stark"; those were not used.
- The source site gives conflicting contact details:
  - `rankhydraulics@gmail.com` in the top bar.
  - `sales@rankhydraulics.com` in multiple footers.
  - `+91 888 999 6666` in the top bar.
  - `+91 88866 69999` in footers and contact cards.
- The source says "Since 25 Years", but also says established in 1999. The rebuild uses "since 1999" to avoid stale date math.
- The source displays `99.5% Customer Satisfaction`; the rebuild does not use it because there is no supporting evidence in the source.
- The source appointment form points to FormSubmit on the home page and a placeholder script URL on the contact page. The rebuild uses a static `mailto:` flow so the project remains standalone.
